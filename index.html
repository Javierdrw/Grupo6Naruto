What is Naruto

Naruto (ナルト, romanized as NARUTO) is a manga series created by Masashi Kishimoto that was serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump. Met with immediate popularity following its initial publication in 1999, the series went on to receive an anime adaptation that commenced broadcasting in 2002.

History of Naruto

Naruto tells the story of a teenage ninja named Naruto Uzumaki, whose parents died in the attack of the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox named Kurama. Naruto aspires to become Hokage (leader of his village) with the aim of being recognized as someone important within the village and among his peers.

Total chapters

The manga concluded in 2014 with a total of 700 chapters. The total number of print copies sold globally exceeds 250 million, and even now NARUTO events, games, merchandise, and more continue to be created and released for fans to enjoy— a testament to the series’ enduring influence and popularity around the world.
