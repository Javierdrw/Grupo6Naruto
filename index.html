Naruto

Naruto (ナルト, romanized as NARUTO) is a manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto, which was later adapted into an anime series. It tells the story of a teenage ninja named Naruto Uzumaki, whose parents died in the attack of the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox named Kurama. Naruto aspires to become Hokage (leader of his village) with the aim of being recognized as someone important within the village and among his peers.
